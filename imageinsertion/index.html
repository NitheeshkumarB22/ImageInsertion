ANIME


ONEPIECE

DESCRIPTION:
"One Piece" is a Japanese manga and anime series created by Eiichiro Oda, following the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his Straw Hat Pirates as they search for the legendary treasure known as the "One Piece" to become the next King of the Pirates.


DRAGONBALL

DESCRIPTION:
Dragon Ball is a Japanese manga series that was later adapted into an anime, following the adventures of Son Goku as he trains in martial arts and searches for the mystical Dragon Balls.


KAIJU NO 8

DESCRIPTION:
Kaiju No. 8 is a Japanese manga series about Kafka Hibino, a man who gains the ability to transform into a powerful kaiju (monster) after a small kaiju enters his body.


I reincarnated as a slime

DESCRIPTION:
"That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime" is a Japanese light novel series, later adapted into an anime and manga, that follows Satoru Mikami, a 37-year-old office worker who is murdered and reincarnated in a fantasy world as a slime.


BLEACH

DESCRIPTION:
Bleach is a popular anime series, based on the manga by Tite Kubo, that follows the adventures of Ichigo Kurosaki, a high school student who gains the powers of a Soul Reaper (a death personification).


7TH PRINCE

DESCRIPTION:
"I Was Reincarnated as the 7th Prince so I Can Take My Time Perfecting My Magical Ability" is a Japanese light novel series where a mage, lacking talent and lineage, dies and is reborn as the seventh prince of the Saloum Kingdom, Lloyd.


MHA

DESCRIPTION:
My Hero Academia is a Japanese superhero manga series written and illustrated by Kōhei Horikoshi, later adapted into an anime. It's set in a world where most people possess superpowers called "Quirks," and follows the journey of Izuku Midoriya, a boy born without a Quirk who dreams of becoming a hero.


NARUTO

DESCRIPTION:
Naruto Uzumaki is the titular protagonist of the Japanese manga and anime series "Naruto". He is a young ninja from the Hidden Leaf Village who dreams of becoming the Hokage, the village's leader, and earning the respect of his peers. Naruto is known for his energetic, optimistic, and sometimes reckless personality, as well as his determination and perseverance despite facing adversity.


SOLOLEVELING

DESCRIPTION:
Solo Leveling is an anime series about Sung Jinwoo, initially the world's weakest hunter, who gains the ability to level up like a video game character after a near-death experience in a dangerous dungeon. He embarks on a journey to discover the secrets behind his newfound powers and the dungeons that spawn monsters, ultimately becoming a powerful force in a world threatened by mysterious gates and powerful creatures.


POKEMON

DESCRIPTION:
he Pokémon anime is a Japanese animated series adapted from the popular video game franchise. It follows the adventures of Ash Ketchum, a young boy who dreams of becoming a Pokémon Master, and his partner Pikachu. Together, they travel through various regions, battling gym leaders, competing in Pokémon Leagues, and encountering a diverse cast of characters and rival trainers.
